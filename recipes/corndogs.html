Corn Dog

Description

This is a recepie of corn dog, easy to make and super delicious snack food.

Ingridients

  • Cornmeal and flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Beef frankfurters

Steps

  1. Make the batter by combining the dry ingredients, then stirring in the milk and egg.
  2. Skewer the frankfurters and roll in the batter.
  3. Fry the corn dogs in hot oil until lightly browned.
